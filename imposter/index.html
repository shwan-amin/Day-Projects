Instructions

Before playing, you must enter the number of players, which is a minimum of 3, and a maximum of 10.

Playing:
1. Assign each person playing a player number.
2. Each person must take the device and read the card which will reveal their fruit.
3. There will be 1 imposter who will be told that they are imposter on their card.
4. The group then must have players take turns, saying a single word to hint at what the fruit is.
5. The group decides how many rounds must be played before they decide to vote.


Voting:
1. If a majority vote is on the imposter, then the civilians win!
2. If the imposter is voted out, but they correctly guess the fruit, they win!
3. If the civilians wrongly vote on another civilian, then the imposter wins!

Good Luck!
